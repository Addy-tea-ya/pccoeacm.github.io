CESA Core Team

The Computer Engineering Students Association (CESA) proactively organises all the PCCOE ACM Chapter events. With the assistance of the Student Development and Welfare coordinator and our experienced faculty mentors , our team of power packed young minds who are immensely passionate about Computing and dedicatedly leading towards our mission to build a community which will be able to use their skills to address the problems of tomorrow through the power of technological advancements and bring synergy between academics and extra-curricular activities.

PCCOE ACM

Set up in 2012, the PCCOE ACM Student Chapter is one of the best Student Chapter in Pune. Year by year, the Chapter strives to increase the number of events, and the participation of students. With the guidance of the faculty sponsors, and the enthusiastic volunteer and participating students, the Chapter which is now in its eighth year is improving rapidly on all counts.
