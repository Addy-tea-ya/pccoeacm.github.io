ANANTYA

Anantya is a technical event organized by the Computer Engineering Students Association(CESA) of PCCOE. Our chapter is awarded the runner up for the best ACM student chapter in India, surpassing all IIT's, NIT's and many other premier institutes. The theme for our event is "Evolution of Technology"
