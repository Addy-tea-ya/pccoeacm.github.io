Welcome to PCube

The PCube program is a platform where students contribute to research and projects, being conducted by the faculty and other fellow students. PCube offers a platform to:
1. Build meaningful connections with faculty and fellow students who share similar research and career interests.
2. Applying classroom learning to real life problems and research.
3. Gaining practical skills and learning more about various fields.
4. Patent inventions, co-author papers,launch a startup,the possibilities are endless.
