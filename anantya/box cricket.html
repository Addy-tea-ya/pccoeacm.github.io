Event Details

About :

Here is the most popular game of INDIA, Box Cricket ! Come with your team of 6 and show your team game.

Prizes :

1st:-  1500 + Certificate

2nd:-  750 + Certificate

General Rules :

  1. Knockout matches.
  2. ONLY FOR PCCOE STUDENTS.
  3. 5 overs match
  4. One bowler can bowl maximum of 2 overs
  5. No video proofing is allowed.
  6. Bats and ball will be provided, you can carry your own bats if your wish to.
  7. No arguments will be entertained with the umpire
  8. Umpire decision will be final

Batting Rules :

  1. One leg must be inside the line then only runs will be granted
  2. There is no runs behind the stumps
  3. If batsman hits six he/she will be consider as out
  4. Other rules related to boundaries will be conveyed on spot

Bowling Rules :

  1. Underarm bowling Only
  2. For WIDE and NO BALL , 1 run will be given without FREEHIT
  3. General rules for wide and no balls are applicable
  4. bowler is not allowed to take run up for the bowling

mode of dismissals :

  1. six out
  2. Caught out
  3. One tip one hand dismissal
  4. Hit wicket will be considered out
  5. Run out
  6. Bowled out

*ALL OTHER RULES WILL BE CONVEYED TO TEAMS DURING EVENT *
